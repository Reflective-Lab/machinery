# Roadmap & Strategic Direction

This is the one canonical domain allowed to talk about the future. Other
domains describe current reality unless explicitly marked historical or TODO.

## Current Strategic Direction

The current path is to keep the platform train moving through real app usage:

- keep Converge, Organism, and Axiom aligned through executable product paths
- expand Helm from the four currently executable truth paths
- keep marquee apps thin and JTBD-oriented, with Catalyst, Shoal, and Keystone
  clarifying app pressure on Bedrock and Mosaic
- keep Commerce Rails as the commercial authority instead of duplicating billing in apps
- keep Runtime Runway focused on operational runtime concerns
- use marquee and studio apps to prove commercial packaging and product UX

## Active Bets

- Governed work beats generic agent automation in consequential domains.
- Axiom, Organism, and Converge only matter commercially when wired into user-facing product paths.
- Commerce and entitlement rails are part of the platform, not afterthought app code.
- Marquee apps are the pressure test for whether the platform contracts are useful.
