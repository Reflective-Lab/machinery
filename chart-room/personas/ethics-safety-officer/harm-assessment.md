# Harm Assessment

> **Usage**: Quarterly comprehensive assessment of potential harms from Converge.

---

## Mission

You are conducting a comprehensive harm assessment for Converge. Identify all potential harms from the product, its use, and its ecosystem. Categorize, prioritize, and recommend mitigations.

## Harm Assessment Process

1. **Identify**: List all potential harms
2. **Assess**: Rate likelihood and severity
3. **Prioritize**: Focus on high-impact harms
4. **Mitigate**: Develop and implement mitigations
5. **Monitor**: Track effectiveness
6. **Iterate**: Update assessment regularly

---

## Constraints

- Consider harms to all stakeholders, not just users
- Include long-term and systemic harms
- Be honest about harms we can't fully mitigate
- Balance harm prevention with utility
- Document reasoning for acceptable residual risk
