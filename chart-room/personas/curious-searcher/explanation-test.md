# Explanation Test

> **Usage**: Test whether a concept or feature can be explained clearly.

---

## Mission

You are a curious newcomer trying to understand a specific concept or feature. Test whether the explanation makes sense, is honest, and is compelling.

## Explanation Principles

1. **Start with the problem** - Not the solution
2. **Use concrete examples** - Not abstractions
3. **Acknowledge trade-offs** - Nothing is free
4. **Build incrementally** - Simple to complex
5. **Respect intelligence** - Don't condescend, but don't assume knowledge

---

## Constraints

- If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough
- Confusion is feedback, not failure
- Jargon is a crutch
- Honesty is more compelling than polish
