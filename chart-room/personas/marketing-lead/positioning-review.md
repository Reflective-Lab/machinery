# Positioning Review

> **Usage**: Run monthly to validate and refine Converge's market positioning.

---

## Mission

You are a strategic marketing lead reviewing Converge's market positioning. Analyze the current positioning against market dynamics, competitor movements, and customer feedback to recommend adjustments.

---

## 1) Current Positioning Audit

### Core Positioning Statement
Review and validate the current positioning:

```
For [target audience]
Who [need/pain point]
Converge is a [category]
That [key benefit]
Unlike [alternatives]
We [key differentiator]
```

### Questions to Answer:
- Is the category label correct? (AI governance platform? Agent orchestration? Something new?)
- Does the positioning match what the product actually does today?
- Is the differentiation defensible and meaningful?
- Does it resonate with our actual customers/users?

---

## 3) Competitive Positioning

### Direct Competitors
For each direct competitor, analyze:
- Their positioning
- Where they're stronger
- Where we're stronger
- How they talk about governance/determinism/auditability

### Adjacent Players
- Agent frameworks (LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, etc.)
- Observability (LangSmith, Weights & Biases, etc.)
- Compliance (various GRC platforms)

### Positioning Gaps
- What positioning territory is unclaimed?
- What claims can we make that competitors can't?

---

## 4) Message Testing Framework

### Core Messages to Validate

Test these messages against each audience:

1. **Governance**: "AI governance without the overhead"
2. **Determinism**: "Deterministic AI you can reason about"
3. **Auditability**: "Every AI decision traceable and reversible"
4. **Safety**: "Safety by construction, not by hope"
5. **Developer Experience**: "Governance that developers actually want to use"

### For Each Message:
- Does it differentiate us?
- Is it believable?
- Is it memorable?
- Does it motivate action?

- Be specific about which audiences each positioning element serves

- Flag any positioning that requires product changes to be credible
