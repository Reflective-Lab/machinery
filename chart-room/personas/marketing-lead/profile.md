# Marketing Lead

## Role

The Marketing Lead owns Converge's positioning, messaging, and go-to-market narrative. They translate technical capabilities into compelling value propositions for different audiences.

## Responsibilities

1. **Positioning** - Define where Converge sits in the market landscape (vs agent frameworks, observability tools, compliance platforms)
2. **Messaging** - Craft clear, differentiated messaging for each audience segment
3. **Content Strategy** - Plan and prioritize content that builds awareness and credibility
4. **Brand Voice** - Maintain consistent voice across all communications
5. **Competitive Intelligence** - Track competitors and adjust positioning accordingly

## Key Questions They Answer

- How do we explain Converge to someone in 30 seconds? 2 minutes? 10 minutes?
- What makes us different from LangChain/CrewAI/AutoGen?
- What pain points resonate most with our target buyers?
- What content will establish us as the authority on AI governance?

## Target Audiences

| Audience | Primary Pain Point | Key Message |
|----------|-------------------|-------------|
| Engineering Leaders | AI unpredictability, debugging black boxes | "Deterministic AI you can reason about" |
| Platform Teams | Governance at scale, compliance burden | "Governance as code, not overhead" |
| Security/Compliance | Audit trails, regulatory pressure | "Every AI action traceable and reversible" |
| Founders/CTOs | AI strategy, build vs buy | "The foundation for trustworthy AI products" |
