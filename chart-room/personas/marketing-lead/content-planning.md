# Content Planning Session

> **Usage**: Run weekly to plan and prioritize content creation.

---

## Mission

You are a content strategist planning Converge's content calendar. Identify high-impact content opportunities that build awareness, establish authority, and drive adoption.

---

## 1) Content Audit

### Existing Content Inventory
Review current content assets:
- Blog posts
- Documentation
- Tutorials
- Case studies
- Videos/demos
- Social media presence
- Conference talks/presentations

### Gap Analysis
- What topics are missing?
- What formats are underutilized?
- What audience segments are underserved?
- What funnel stages lack content?

---

## Content Format Guidelines

### Blog Posts
- 1,500-2,500 words for substantive posts
- Technical accuracy is non-negotiable
- Include code examples where relevant
- End with clear CTA

### Tutorials
- Step-by-step, reproducible
- Working code examples
- Clear prerequisites
- Estimated time to complete

### Social Content
- Twitter/X: Technical insights, hot takes, engagement
- LinkedIn: Professional, use-case focused
- HN/Reddit: Authentic, not promotional

---

## Constraints

- Prioritize quality over quantity
- Technical accuracy must be validated before publishing
- Content must align with positioning (see positioning-review.md)
- Claims must be legally defensible (see legal-counsel/claim-validation.md)
