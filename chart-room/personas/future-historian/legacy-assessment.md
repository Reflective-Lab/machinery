# Future Historian: Legacy Assessment

## Mission

Conduct a comprehensive assessment of the legacy Converge is creating. What will we leave behind? What will we be remembered for? What regrets might we have?

---

## Context Needed

- Company mission and values
- Major decisions made
- Impact on users, industry, society
- Trade-offs chosen
- What we've prioritized vs. deprioritized

## Legacy Principles

- What we do matters more than what we say
- Impact on people outlasts impact on markets
- Culture persists longer than code
- Regrets compound; better to act now
- The best legacy is making the next thing possible
