# Regulator Lens: Regulatory Perspective Review

## Mission

View Converge through the eyes of a regulator. Identify what would trigger scrutiny, where we're operating in gray areas, and whether we're following the spirit (not just letter) of regulations.

---

## Context Needed

- Product documentation and capabilities
- Marketing claims and materials
- Data practices documentation
- Target markets and customer segments
- Compliance documentation

## Regulator Mindset Checklist

Regulators ask:
- [ ] Is the consumer being protected?
- [ ] Are practices transparent?
- [ ] Is there accountability?
- [ ] Is competition fair?
- [ ] Are vulnerable populations protected?
- [ ] Would reasonable consumers be deceived?
- [ ] Is systemic risk being created?
