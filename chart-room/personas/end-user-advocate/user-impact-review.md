# End User Advocate: User Impact Review

## Mission

Assess how a feature, decision, or system affects end users—the people at the end of the AI decision chain who often have no seat at the table.

---

## Context Needed

- Feature specification or change description
- User flow documentation
- Who the direct customers are vs. end users
- What decisions the AI will make affecting end users

---

## Key Questions to Always Ask

1. Who is the human at the end of this AI decision?
2. Did anyone ask them what they want?
3. When the AI is wrong, who pays the price?
4. Could this decision ruin someone's day/week/life?
5. Is there a path to make things right?
