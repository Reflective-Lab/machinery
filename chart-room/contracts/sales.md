# Sales Phase Contracts

Responsibilities during customer engagement and sales.

---

## Sales Engineer

**Role**: Responsible for technical sales

### Responsibilities
- Run demos
- Qualify prospects technically
- Design POCs
- Handle objections
- Support deal process

### Gates
- [ ] Demo environment ready
- [ ] Technical qualification complete
- [ ] POC designed and agreed

### Deliverables
- Demo scripts
- Technical qualification reports
- POC proposals
- Objection handling

### Collaboration
- **With Marketing Lead**: Positioning alignment
- **With System Architect**: Technical questions
- **With Legal Counsel**: Contract terms

---

## Marketing Lead

**Role**: Consulted for positioning

### Responsibilities
- Ensure sales aligns with positioning
- Provide competitive intelligence
- Support messaging consistency

### Gates
- [ ] Sales materials align with positioning

### Deliverables
- Battle cards
- Competitive positioning

### Collaboration
- **With Sales Engineer**: Alignment
- **With Developer Advocate**: Content support

---

## Legal Counsel

**Role**: Responsible for contract terms

### Responsibilities
- Review contracts
- Approve terms
- Manage legal risk

### Gates
- [ ] Contracts reviewed and approved
- [ ] Terms are acceptable

### Deliverables
- Contract review
- Terms guidance

### Collaboration
- **With Sales Engineer**: Deal structure
- **With Founder**: Strategic deals

---

## Investor

**Role**: Consulted for deal strategy

### Responsibilities
- Advise on strategic deals
- Provide market perspective
- Review significant commitments

### Gates
- [ ] Strategic alignment of major deals

### Deliverables
- Strategic feedback

### Collaboration
- **With Founder**: Strategic decisions
- **With Sales Engineer**: Deal structure

---

## Ethics & Safety Officer

**Role**: Consulted for customer ethics

### Responsibilities
- Review customer use cases
- Flag concerning applications
- Ensure ethical customer selection

### Gates
- [ ] Customer use case is ethical

### Deliverables
- Use case ethics review

### Collaboration
- **With Sales Engineer**: Use case review
- **With Legal Counsel**: Acceptable use

---

## SRE / Operations

**Role**: Consulted for capacity

### Responsibilities
- Assess capacity for new customers
- Plan for onboarding load
- Ensure operational readiness

### Gates
- [ ] Capacity available for new customer

### Deliverables
- Capacity assessment

### Collaboration
- **With Sales Engineer**: Deployment planning
- **With System Architect**: Architecture fit

---

## End User Advocate

**Role**: Consulted for user impact

### Responsibilities
- Consider end users of customer's AI
- Ensure customer will use responsibly
- Advocate for affected parties

### Gates
- [ ] End user impact considered

### Deliverables
- User impact feedback

### Collaboration
- **With Ethics & Safety**: Overlap on ethics
- **With Sales Engineer**: Customer selection

---

## Phase Completion Checklist

Before closing deal:

