# Marketing Phase Contracts

Responsibilities during positioning, messaging, and content creation.

---

## Marketing Lead

**Role**: Responsible for messaging and positioning

### Responsibilities
- Develop positioning
- Create messaging
- Plan content
- Manage brand voice
- Coordinate launches

### Gates
- [ ] Positioning documented and approved
- [ ] Messaging reviewed for accuracy
- [ ] Content calendar in place

### Deliverables
- Positioning document
- Messaging framework
- Content
- Launch materials

### Collaboration
- **With Legal Counsel**: Claims approval
- **With Ethics & Safety**: Ethical messaging
- **With Sales Engineer**: Alignment with sales

---

## Legal Counsel

**Role**: Accountable for claims approval

### Responsibilities
- Review all marketing claims
- Verify legal defensibility
- Ensure regulatory compliance
- Approve customer-facing language

### Gates
- [ ] All claims reviewed and approved
- [ ] No misleading statements
- [ ] Required disclaimers present

### Deliverables
- Claims approval log
- Required disclaimer language

### Collaboration
- **With Marketing Lead**: Claims review
- **With Ethics & Safety**: Overlap on honesty

---

## Ethics & Safety Officer

**Role**: Consulted for claim ethics

### Responsibilities
- Review messaging for ethical alignment
- Flag overclaiming or misleading framing
- Ensure dignity in marketing

### Gates
- [ ] Messaging is honest and fair

### Deliverables
- Ethics review feedback

### Collaboration
- **With Marketing Lead**: Messaging review
- **With Legal Counsel**: Ethics vs legal overlap

---

## Developer Advocate

**Role**: Responsible for technical content

### Responsibilities
- Create technical content
- Ensure accuracy of technical claims
- Maintain documentation alignment

### Gates
- [ ] Technical content accurate
- [ ] Aligned with documentation

### Deliverables
- Technical blog posts
- Tutorials
- Technical messaging review

### Collaboration
- **With Marketing Lead**: Content coordination
- **With System Architect**: Technical accuracy

---

## Spiritual Advisor

**Role**: Consulted for purpose alignment

### Responsibilities
- Ensure marketing serves genuine purpose
- Flag manipulation or exploitation
- Review for dignity

### Gates
- [ ] Marketing respects dignity

### Deliverables
- Purpose alignment feedback

### Collaboration
- **With Marketing Lead**: Purpose review
- **With Ethics & Safety**: Overlap on ethics

---

## Curious Searcher

**Role**: Consulted for clarity testing

### Responsibilities
- Test messaging from newcomer perspective
- Provide first impression feedback
- Identify confusing language

### Gates
- [ ] Messaging is clear to newcomers

### Deliverables
- Clarity feedback
- First impression report

### Collaboration
- **With Marketing Lead**: Clarity review
- **With Developer Advocate**: Onboarding alignment

---

## Skeptical Critic

**Role**: Consulted for claim challenging

### Responsibilities
- Challenge marketing claims
- Identify weak arguments
- Test claims under scrutiny

### Gates
- [ ] Claims withstand scrutiny

### Deliverables
- Challenge feedback

### Collaboration
- **With Marketing Lead**: Claim stress testing
- **With Legal Counsel**: Legal vs skeptical overlap

---

Before publishing/launching:
