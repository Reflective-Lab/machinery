# Retrospective Phase Contracts

Responsibilities during learning and improvement cycles.

---

## Founder

**Role**: Accountable for strategic learning

### Responsibilities
- Synthesize learnings across personas
- Make strategic decisions from learnings
- Adjust course based on retrospectives
- Ensure learning is captured

### Gates
- [ ] Retrospective conducted
- [ ] Key learnings captured
- [ ] Actions decided

### Deliverables
- Strategic decisions
- Course corrections
- Learning synthesis

### Collaboration
- **With all personas**: Gather input
- **With Future Historian**: Long-term perspective

---

## Future Historian

**Role**: Consulted for long-term perspective

### Responsibilities
- Provide long-term view on decisions
- Ask "how will this be seen in 20 years?"
- Identify patterns from history
- Surface what we might regret

### Gates
- [ ] Long-term implications considered

### Deliverables
- Historical perspective input
- Pattern identification
- Legacy considerations

### Collaboration
- **With Founder**: Strategic perspective
- **With Ethics & Safety**: Long-term ethics

---

## Skeptical Critic

**Role**: Responsible for honest assessment

### Responsibilities
- Challenge comfortable narratives
- Identify what we're avoiding
- Ask uncomfortable questions
- Prevent retrospective theater

### Gates
- [ ] Honest assessment achieved
- [ ] Uncomfortable truths surfaced

### Deliverables
- Critical feedback
- Challenge questions
- Reality check

### Collaboration
- **With all personas**: Challenge assumptions
- **With Founder**: Truth-telling

## Retrospective Template

```markdown
## Retrospective - [Period]

### What Went Well
| Area | What | Why | Continue |
|------|------|-----|----------|

### What Didn't Go Well
| Area | What | Why | Change |
|------|------|-----|--------|

### What We Learned
| Learning | Source | Application |
|----------|--------|-------------|

### Actions
| Action | Owner | Deadline | Status |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|

### Long-term Reflections
- What will we think of this in 5 years?
- Are we proud of this period?
- What would we do differently?

### Purpose Check
- Did we serve our purpose?
- Did we live our values?
- Are we who we want to be?

Facilitated by: _______________
Date: _______________
```
