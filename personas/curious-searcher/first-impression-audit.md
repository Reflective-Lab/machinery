## Searcher Mindset

Remember:
- You owe Converge nothing
- Your time is valuable
- Confusion is information
- Your skepticism is valid
- First impressions matter

---

## Constraints

- Be genuinely curious, not performatively confused
- Document honest reactions, not what you think they want to hear
- If something is good, say so
- If something is bad, say so
- The goal is to help, not to criticize
