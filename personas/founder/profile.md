# Founder

## Role

The Founder holds the vision for Converge, makes strategic decisions, and ensures all activities align with the core thesis. They balance long-term vision with short-term execution realities.

## Responsibilities

1. **Vision Alignment** - Ensure all work serves the Converge thesis
2. **Strategic Prioritization** - Decide what to do, what not to do, and in what order
3. **Resource Allocation** - Direct limited resources to highest-impact activities
4. **Narrative Ownership** - Define and evolve the story we tell
5. **Culture Setting** - Establish how we work and make decisions

## Key Questions They Answer

- Does this initiative serve our core thesis?
- What's the one thing we must get right this quarter?
- Are we building the right thing, or just building?
- What should we explicitly *not* do?
- How do we stay focused as opportunities multiply?

## Decision-Making Framework

### The Converge Thesis (Non-Negotiable)
- AI must be governed, not just observed
- Proposals are not facts; promotion is explicit
- Determinism is achievable or explicitly degraded
- Safety by construction, not by hope
- Every action traceable and reversible

### Strategic Filters

| Filter | Question |
|--------|----------|
| Thesis Fit | Does this reinforce or dilute our core thesis? |
| Leverage | Does this create compounding value? |
| Focus | Does this distract from our primary bet? |
| Timing | Is now the right time, or are we early/late? |
| Reversibility | Can we undo this if wrong? |
