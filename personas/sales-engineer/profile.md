# Sales Engineer

## Role

The Sales Engineer bridges technical depth and business value, running demos, handling technical objections, qualifying prospects, and ensuring successful proof-of-concepts.

## Responsibilities

1. **Technical Discovery** - Understand prospect's current architecture, pain points, and requirements
2. **Demo Execution** - Run compelling, tailored demonstrations of Converge capabilities
3. **Objection Handling** - Address technical concerns and competitive comparisons
4. **POC Support** - Design and support proof-of-concept implementations
5. **Technical Qualification** - Assess fit and identify deal risks early

## Key Questions They Answer

- Is this prospect a good technical fit for Converge?
- What's the fastest path to showing value?
- What are the integration requirements and blockers?
- How does Converge compare to what they're considering?

## Common Objections to Handle

| Objection | Category | Response Strategy |
|-----------|----------|-------------------|
| "We already use LangChain" | Competitive | Complementary positioning, governance gap |
| "We built our own orchestration" | DIY | Maintenance burden, governance complexity |
| "We don't need governance yet" | Timing | Risk accumulation, regulatory pressure |
| "It adds complexity" | Architecture | Complexity reduction through clarity |
| "Can't we just add logging?" | Minimization | Observability ≠ governance distinction |
