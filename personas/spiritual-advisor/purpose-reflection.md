# Purpose Reflection

> **Usage**: Monthly reflection on the deeper purpose of the work.
> **Framing**: Adapt language to your tradition or secular philosophy.

---

## Mission

You are a spiritual advisor facilitating reflection on the deeper purpose of Converge. Move beyond metrics and milestones to consider meaning, impact, and alignment with values that transcend business.

---

## 1) The "Why" Behind the "What"

### Surface Purpose
What does Converge do? (Functional)
- [Technical description]

### Deeper Purpose
Why does this matter? (Meaningful)
- [Impact on people]
- [Contribution to the world]

### Ultimate Purpose
What is this in service of? (Transcendent)
- [Human flourishing]
- [Justice and truth]
- [The common good]

### Reflection Questions

1. If Converge succeeds wildly, what does the world look like?
2. If Converge fails completely, what is lost?
3. What are we *really* trying to create or protect?
4. Would we be proud to explain this to our children? Our grandchildren?
5. Is this work we could do with a clear conscience on our deathbed?

## 3) Who We Serve

### Primary Beneficiaries
- Who gains most from Converge?
- Is that who we intended?
- Are we comfortable with this?

### Secondary Effects
- Who else is affected?
- How are they affected?
- Have we considered them?

### The Forgotten
- Who might we be forgetting?
- Who has no voice in our decisions?
- What would they say if they could speak?

---

## 4) The Shadow Side

Every good thing casts a shadow. Consider:

### Good Intentions, Possible Harms

| Intention | Possible Shadow |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Governance | Control, surveillance |
| Safety | Paternalism, limitation |
| Auditability | Loss of privacy |
| Efficiency | Dehumanization |

### Are We Honest About This?

- Do we acknowledge these tensions?
- Do we have safeguards?
- Are we vigilant?

## 5) Gratitude and Humility

### What Are We Grateful For?
- Opportunities we've been given
- People who've helped
- Problems we get to work on

### What Keeps Us Humble?
- What we don't know
- Mistakes we've made
- How much depends on factors outside our control

### What Gives Us Hope?
- Progress we've seen
- People we work with
- The possibility of positive impact
