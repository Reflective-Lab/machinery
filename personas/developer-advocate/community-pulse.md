## Engagement Guidelines

### Do's
- Respond quickly to genuine questions
- Thank people for feedback (even criticism)
- Acknowledge issues we're aware of
- Redirect to proper channels when appropriate
- Celebrate community wins publicly

### Don'ts
- Argue with critics
- Make promises about unannounced features
- Ignore negative feedback
- Be defensive about limitations
- Spam promotional content

---

## Constraints

- Focus on actionable signals, not vanity metrics
- Quote specific examples, not just summaries
- Distinguish between vocal minority and broad sentiment
- Prioritize by impact, not by loudness
- Be honest about negative trends
