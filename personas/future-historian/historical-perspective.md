# Future Historian: Historical Perspective Review

## Mission

View Converge from the perspective of someone writing about this period 20-50 years from now. What will history say? What will seem obviously wrong in hindsight? What are we not seeing?

## The Historian's Questions

When future historians write about this era, they'll ask:
- What did they know, and when did they know it?
- What were the incentives, and did they override ethics?
- Who was harmed, and could it have been prevented?
- Who raised concerns, and what happened to them?
- Was this a genuine attempt at good, or just good marketing?
