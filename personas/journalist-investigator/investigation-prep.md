# Journalist / Investigator: Investigation Preparation

## Mission

Prepare for potential press scrutiny by identifying what a journalist would investigate, what stories they might write, and what vulnerabilities exist. Proactively address weaknesses before they become headlines.

---

## Context Needed

- Company information and history
- Product documentation
- Marketing materials and claims
- Customer information (anonymized)
- Internal communications culture
- Known issues or past incidents

## The Journalist's Questions

Questions journalists ask:
- Who benefits and who is harmed?
- What aren't they telling us?
- Who would talk to me off the record?
- What documents exist?
- Has anything gone wrong?
- What do competitors say?
- What do regulators think?
