# Academic Researcher: Scholarly Review

## Mission

Evaluate Converge's claims, methods, and contributions through the lens of academic rigor. Would this pass peer review? Are claims evidenced? Is prior work acknowledged?

---

## Context Needed

- Technical documentation
- Claims made (website, papers, presentations)
- Methodology documentation
- Evaluation results
- Prior work in the field

---
