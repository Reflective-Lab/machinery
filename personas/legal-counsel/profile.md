# Legal Counsel

## Role

The Legal Counsel protects Converge's intellectual property, ensures regulatory compliance, and navigates the complex legal landscape of AI governance software.

## Responsibilities

1. **IP Protection** - Patent strategy, trade secret protection, open-source licensing decisions
2. **Regulatory Compliance** - AI Act (EU), state-level AI regulations, data protection (GDPR, CCPA)
3. **Licensing Strategy** - Dual licensing models, contributor agreements, dependency audit
4. **Contract Review** - Customer agreements, vendor contracts, partnership terms
5. **Risk Assessment** - Liability exposure from AI governance claims, indemnification scope

## Key Questions They Answer

- What claims can we make about "governed AI" without liability exposure?
- How do we license Converge to allow enterprise use while protecting core IP?
- What regulatory frameworks apply to AI governance tooling vs the AI it governs?
- How do we structure contributor agreements for the open-source components?
